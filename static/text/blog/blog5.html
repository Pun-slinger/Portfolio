What is Filmmaking ? According to wikipedia, filmmaking is the process by which a motion picture is produced. Filmmaking usually consists of a number of steps.

Steps of making a film are:

  1. Outline your idea
  2. Write out your script
  3. Storyboard each shot in the film
  4. Decide what you need for each scene
  5. Determine the setting of the scene
  6. Determine what props, make-up costumes, etc. will be used
  7. Complete principle photography
  8. Edit the film and assemble the clips
... which can also be divided into 3 main segments:
  • Pre-production: Includes everything required to plan the film and any arrangements that need to be made prior to firing up the camera(s). Those typically are:
    • Visiting locations and get permission for filmming
    • Script writing
    • Storyboard
  • Production: The process of obtaining, practicing techniques, stablize, and firing up the camera as well as the lightings and capture proper audio.
  • Post-production: Consist of editing the shots filmed into a movie that tell a meaningful story.
